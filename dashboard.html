Takeaway

Sizing & fit issues (clusters 0, 1, 4 — 1,264 reviews) drive the majority of complaints, followed by fabric-quality concerns (clusters 2, 3 — 1,106 reviews).

Business Recommendations

Turning model output into decisions the business can act on

1

Fix sizing guidance

Sizing/fit spans 3 of 5 complaint clusters (1,264 of 2,370 negative reviews) — improving size charts directly targets the #1 complaint driver.

2

Audit fabric suppliers

"Cheap", "thin", "poor material" complaints cluster around specific departments, pointing to a sourcing issue rather than a company-wide one.

3

Prioritize fixes by department

Linking each cluster back to Department Name lets merchandising teams focus resources where complaint volume is highest.

4

Route Neutral reviews for human review

Neutral (3-star) reviews are the hardest to classify automatically (F1 = 0.41) — flag them for manual triage or a stronger model in production.
